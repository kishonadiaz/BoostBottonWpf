This is Boost Button!
This Application allows you to turn on your Ultimate Performace plan in windows 10
for more infomation click here
Warning!!!!!
Ultimate Performance is a double edge sword it will not harm the short line of your computer, but in long term use, it can cause part degradation. Put it this way it like driving your car at its maximum speed, for as long as this mode is on everything is running at 100% of its full capacity can cause the computer to run Hotter!
To use this widget you can push the button in the center this button activtes Ultimate Performace and press it again to turn it off
To move the widget grab the title bar and move it to where you would like
This widget auto hides into the tray you can turn off tray mode if you would like
To open setting You can change your settings here
In tray mode you can turn on and off Boost mode
